About - Toa Carney

Introduction

My name is Toa Carney. I was born in the UK but I am from New Zealand. I have been living in New Zealand for about 5 years. I live in Palmerston North and I am currently attending Freyberg High Schoool

Hobbies

One thing I really like doing is electronics prototyping. I like to build electrical devices out of second-hand components. In addition to circuit building, I also like to tinker with the Arduino platform. Arduino is an open-source platform which merges hardware design with software design. It allows you to build a circuit and automate that circuit with Input/Output pins.

Some of the things I have built are:

  • Electromagnetic Feild Meter
  • Colour Organ
  • An inkjet printer that plays MIDI
  • Text Analysing Mood Light (Entered into the Manawatu Science and Technology Fair 2014)

Extras

I am a keen music person. I have been playing music since 2010. I am currently learning the Alto Saxophone at Freyberg High School.

One other thing I am in to is aviation. My uncle is a Pilot and he occasionaly takes me flying in his aircraft.
